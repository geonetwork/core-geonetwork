GeoNetwork's purpose is:

  • To improve access to and integrated use of spatial data and information
  • To support decision making
  • To promote multidisciplinary approaches to sustainable development
  • To enhance understanding of the benefits of geographic information

Access this catalogue using the following services:
  • Catalogue Service for the web (CSW) de l'OGC
  • Open Archive Initiative (OAI-PMH)
  • Z39.50
  • OpenSearch

GeoNetwork opensource allows to easily share geographically referenced thematic information between different organizations. For more information please contact
